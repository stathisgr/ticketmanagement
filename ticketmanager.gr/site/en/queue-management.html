Alpha Queue Management covers the simple need of issuing a priority number with one key press, yet extends into a full queue-management environment: multiple service points, a public waiting display, statistics, history and configuration. It automates ticket issuing, calling customers/visitors, showing the current number on a waiting display, and monitoring through a management dashboard and reports.

The price covers one site with unlimited service points. Technical support is not included — if you need it, you can choose an Alpha Online maintenance & support plan.

Full software

Everything an organised waiting line needs

  • Priority number issuing with a single action.
  • Instant ticket printing on a thermal printer.
  • Multiple service points, with no limit.
  • Call the next customer or visitor from each point.
  • Public waiting display for a TV or monitor.
  • Audible & visual notification on each call.

What it offers

Better organised waiting

The service area runs in order, with transparency and less pressure on staff. The customer or visitor gets a priority number, knows they are in line, and is notified by the public display when it is their turn. This reduces the sense of disorder and avoids arguments over “who is next”.

How it works

The system runs over the local network. One computer acts as the central server (usually at the reception or front desk) and the other devices connect through a browser on the same network. Updates happen in real time: as soon as a ticket is issued or a number is called, the relevant screens update automatically.
