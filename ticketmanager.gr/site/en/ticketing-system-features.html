Features

Everything a modern venue needs

Box office, online bookings, seats, payments, QR check-in, reports and tax compliance — in one simple, affordable solution.

Home › Features
🧾

Box-office issuing (POS)

Fast serial issuing: quantity, type, payment, print — in one click. Ideal for general admission.

🪑

Halls & seats

Design halls with rows/columns/aisles. Visual map with available/selected/sold seats.

🌐

Online bookings

Public page: date → event → seats/quantity → details → card payment.

💳

Card payments

At the physical box office and online, for instant checkout.

📲

e-ticket with QR

The ticket arrives by email; the customer shows it from their phone at the entrance.

✅

Entrance control

Scan the QR or number; validity, correct event and double-use checks.

📊

Box office & reports

Sales, revenue, types, cash/card per day, event or hall; CSV export.

🎟️

Ticket types

Regular, reduced, student, child, accessibility, invitations, group packages — each with its own price.

🖨️

Thermal printing

Physical tickets on 58/80mm thermal printers; configurable ticket layout.

🧮

Tax compliance

Ticket-only issuing, integration with a cash register or e-invoicing provider (myDATA).

👥

User roles

Administrator, cashier and gate controller, each with what they need.

🛍️

Products (optional)

Sell programmes, prints or souvenirs at the box office, with proper configuration.
